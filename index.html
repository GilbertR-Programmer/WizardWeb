Rules

We be wizards is a simple game with simple rules... at first. All the rules you need to play are below

1 You are a wizard who knows four spells.
2 You roll 1d4 when you want to cast a spell and cast that spell.
3 If you take four hits you die.
4 At the end of every game session add two new rules, all players must agree on the new rules.
