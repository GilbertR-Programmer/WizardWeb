We Be Wizards!

A ttrpg where ye be wizards

Rules

We be wizards is a simple game with simple rules... at first. All the rules you need to play are below

1 You are a wizard who knows four spells numbered 1-4.
2 To cast a spell roll 1d4, the number you roll determines the spell you cast.
3 If you take four hits you die.
4 At the end of every game session add/change up to two rules, all players must agree on the rules.

Example of play

This example has three players Sally, Dave and Grace who is running the game.

These are the players characters/spell lists

Eric (Sally) Sam (Dave)
1 Conjure Goblin Levitate
2 Lightning Bolt Fireball
3 Create Light Conjure Goblin
4 Create Light Hasten man

The group is exploring a dank cave searching for a magic goblin ruby as part of a longer journey.

In play

Grace (Running game): The cave is dark and damp, with the sound of dripping water echoing through the tunnels. You can barely make out the path ahead, but you can see a glint deeper in the cave. What do you do?

Sally (Playing Eric): We can't see, I'm going to cast conjure flame.

Grace: Ok great please roll your d4 to see which spell you cast.

*Sally rolls 1d4 and gets a 3*

Sally: That's create light.

Grace: You fill the cave with bright light and can now see clearly the goblin ruby, along with three armed goblins guarding it. What do you do?

Dave (Playing Sam): I cast a spell at the goblins!

*Dave rolls 1d4 and gets a 1*

Dave: That's levitate, what happens?

Grace: The goblins begin to levitate

Dave: I run under them and grab the ruby

Grace: Excellent. You grab the ruby, but catch a spear to the back from on of the above goblins.

Dave: Damn I've taken three already, one more and i die.

Grace: I have to save Sam, I cast a spell at the goblins

*Sam rolls 1d4 and gets a 1*

Sam: That's conjure goblin, what happens?

Grace: Now there are four goblins guarding the ruby, one standing next to Sam.

We jump ahead to the end of the session

Here the players have a conversation about the game. They decide they need to be able to heal when they rest and cast spells better.

They add the following rules

5 When you rest you undo one hit taken.
6 When you find yourself in a good position to cast a spell instead roll 2d4 and pick the result you want.
